We are Working world With 3 Years

ANJIN Metal Industries was established in 2021. Its location is Town Noapara, Fakirhat, Bagerhat. The purpose of establishing the company is to meet the demand of domestic aluminum products and export them abroad. The Company will produce all kind of Aluminum Products. Such as Ingots ,Miracle metal in window frames, cooking utensils and embedded in electronics. For architects and engineers, aluminum is a material of choice when constructing buildings and bridges. Renewable energy relies on it for solar panels, wind turbines and cables. Aluminum keeps our food fresh and our medicine safe. And when we travel, we depend on it in our cars, aircraft and trains. For all these purpose ANJIN Metal Industries produce all kind of aluminum products.
